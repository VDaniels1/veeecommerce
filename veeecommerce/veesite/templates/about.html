About Us

Our Company

Founded in 2017, Unicure Pharmacy began as a small, family-run business with a simple mission:
to provide affordable and quality healthcare products to the local community.
Its founder, Dr.Samuel Gichuhi a pharmacist with a vision to bridge the gap in accessible healthcare, opened the first store in Nairobi. The pharmacy quickly gained a reputation for excellent customer service,
reliable pharmaceutical advice, and an extensive range of prescription and
over-the-counter medications. By focusing on customer satisfaction and trust,
Unicure Pharmacy expanded its operations, opening additional branches across the region within its first Biennial.

Our Core Values

Integrity,Professionalism, Excellence and Customer focus. We are proud with our
commitment to providing quality products and services to our customers.

Our Vision

To be a trusted and reliable healthcare partner,
offering accessible and affordable pharmaceutical solutions, ensuring the well-being of our community with compassionate care and innovation.

Our Mission

"To provide safe, affordable, and accessible healthcare products and services to our community.
We are dedicated to delivering high-quality pharmaceuticals, health consultations, and personalized care,
ensuring the well-being of every customer through professionalism, compassion, and integrity."
